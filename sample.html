Wonderful roof terrace

Special and elegant design hotel in the heart of Budapest with unique architectural solutions. You should not miss the roof terrace where you can admire the odd views with great food and drinks or just a coffee and cake. Dessert creations are my favorites. I've been here a few times for a cup of coffee, my soul is always cheering on the environment ... :-)
